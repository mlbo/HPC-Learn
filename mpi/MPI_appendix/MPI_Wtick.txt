NAME
       MPI_Wtick -  Returns the resolution of MPI_Wtime

SYNOPSIS
       #include "mpi.h"
       double MPI_Wtick()

RETURN VALUE
       Time in seconds of the resolution of MPI_Wtime .


NOTES FOR FORTRAN
       This  is  a  function, declared as DOUBLE PRECISION MPI_WTICK() in For-
       tran.


LOCATION
       wtick.c
